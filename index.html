About Us

"AVICENNA" is an international community of people dedicated to yoga and spiritual development. Our platform features lectures and practices from two masters: Dr. Alisher Vasiliev and Master of Qigong and Tibetan Yoga, Yuri Pak.

Founder of the Community

Alexander Melentiev, a certified Kundalini Yoga teacher from the international Amritnam Sarovar school.

Our Masters

Dr. Alisher Vasiliev

Psychoneurologist, PhD (Candidate of Medical Sciences), specialist in Tibetan and Chinese medicine, author, writer, and traveler.

Experience

Dr. Alisher Vasiliev studied under many spiritual teachers, including the Dalai Lama and Sai Baba. He has a long history of international practice (in South America and Austria) and was personally acquainted with Indira Gandhi.

Proprietary System

Based on his accumulated knowledge and experience, Dr. Vasiliev developed a proprietary system aimed at comprehensive body healing, mental development, improving quality of life, and achieving personal goals.

Published Works

Author of 9 books including the "Asian Cycle" in 2 parts, "Meditations on the Chakras," "Mystical Jizzakh," "Gold of the Hindu Kush," "Raspberry Paradise," and the "Venezuelan Cycle" in 3 parts.

Yuri Pak

A follower of the teachings of Tibetan lamas, a teacher of Tibetan, Tien-Shan, and Taoist yoga, Qigong, and meditation with 32 years of teaching experience.

Experience

Founder of the International School of Yoga with over three decades of dedicated practice and teaching.

Retreats and Pilgrimages

The community team also organizes yoga retreats and pilgrimages to sacred places around the planet. Each retreat and pilgrimage is accompanied by masters of yoga, Qigong, and healing.

Over the past year, we have conducted 10 retreats and 2 pilgrimages, including one in Uzbekistan and a second in Malaysia.
